Courses Available

Computer Science & Engineering

Programming (C, C++, Java), data structures, DBMS, networking, web technologies, software engineering.

  • * Duration : 3 years
  • * Intake: 60 seats
  • * Fees : ₹2,000 per year
  • * Eligibility : Class 10 pass with compulsory Mathematics & Science.

Civil Engineering

Structural analysis, building design, surveying, construction materials, hydraulics, environment.

  • * Duration : 3 years
  • * Intake: 60 seats
  • * Fees : ₹2,000 per year
  • * Eligibility : Class 10 pass with compulsory Mathematics & Science.

Mechanical Engineering

Machine Design, Manufacturing Processes, CAD, Workshop Practice, Engines, and Automotive Drawing.

  • * Duration : 3 years
  • * Intake: 60 seats
  • * Fees : ₹2,000 per year
  • * Eligibility : Class 10 pass with compulsory Mathematics & Science.

Electronics & Instrumentation Engineering

Sensors, measurement systems, process control, instrumentation, automation, embedded systems.

  • * Duration : 3 years
  • * Intake: 60 seats
  • * Fees : ₹2,000 per year
  • * Eligibility : Class 10 pass with compulsory Mathematics & Science.

Architecture Assistantship

Architectural drafting, building design principles, materials, CAD/drawing, model‑making.

  • * Duration : 3 years
  • * Intake: 30 seats
  • * Fees : ₹1,540 per year
  • * Eligibility : Class 10 pass with ≥35%, admission via JEXPO/VOCLET

Electronics & Telecommunication Engineering

Electronic circuits, digital communication, transmission systems, networks, microprocessors, mobile/optical systems.

  • * Duration : 3 years
  • * Intake: 60 seats
  • * Fees : ₹2,450 per year
  • * Eligibility : Class 10 pass with compulsory Mathematics & Science.

Pharmacy

Pharmacology, pharmaceutical chemistry, pharmaceutics, dispensing, hospital & community pharmacy, regulations.

  • * Duration : 2 years
  • * Intake: 60 seats
  • * Fees : ₹2,500 per year
  • * Eligibility : Completed Class 12 (10+2) in Science stream with Physics, Chemistry and either Biology or Mathematics (≥50% aggregate and pass in each)
