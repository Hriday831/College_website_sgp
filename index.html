Frequently Asked Question

How to get admission in diploma courses?

Admission is through the JEXPO entrance exam conducted by the West Bengal State Council of Technical and Vocational Education and Skill Development each year.

What are the eligibility criteria for admission?

Candidates must have passed Madhyamik (10th standard) or equivalent with Mathematics, Physical Science, and English to be eligible for admission to diploma courses.

Are hostel facilities available for students?

No, the college does not provides hostel facilities for students, you may opt for nearby private hostels or PG accommodations

What is the course fee structure?

Being a government institution, the fee structure is very affordable, with tuition fees around ₹50 per month and other minimal charges for library, lab, and exam.

Does the college offer placement support?

Yes, the Training and Placement Cell helps students with campus interviews, internships, and soft skill development to enhance job opportunities.

What are the college class timings?

The college typically functions from 10:00 AM to 4:30 PM, and each academic session spans three years, divided into six semesters.

Is lateral entry available for diploma courses?

Yes, students with ITI or 10+2 vocational qualifications can apply for lateral entry directly into the second year through the VOCLET exam.

Is the college recognized by AICTE?

Yes, it is affiliated with the West Bengal State Council of Technical Education and recognized by AICTE, ensuring the validity of its diploma programs.

Are any scholarships or financial aids provided?

Yes, students can apply for government scholarships like OBC, SC/ST, and Kanyashree, as well as NSP schemes based on merit or economic background.

What facilities are available on the campus?

The campus offers classrooms, laboratories, a library, workshops, a computer center, and basic sports facilities to support student learning and overall development.
